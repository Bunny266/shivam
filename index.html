About Me

Hi, I’m Shivam Mishra, a passionate street photographer with a deep love for capturing the raw, unfiltered beauty of everyday life. My journey behind the lens began with a simple curiosity and quickly turned into a lifelong pursuit of storytelling through candid moments and urban narratives. From quiet alleyways to bustling markets, I aim to freeze time — preserving emotions, culture, and character in every frame. I believe the streets speak if you know how to listen — and I try to translate that voice into images that connect, provoke, and inspire. Whether it’s a fleeting glance, dramatic light, or layered composition, I’m always in search of the extraordinary hidden in the ordinary.
