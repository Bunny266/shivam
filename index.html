About Me

SHIVAM Mishra
Graphic Designer | Street Photographer
With over 1.5 years of experience in graphic design and 6 months in street photography, I bring a versatile and creative approach to visual storytelling. My expertise lies in delivering impactful design solutions while capturing candid narratives through the lens. As a graphic designer, I specialize in developing modern, user-centric designs that enhance brand identity and digital communication. My work spans logo creation, social media content, print collateral, and digital layouts—all tailored to meet both aesthetic and strategic objectives. In parallel, my passion for street photography allows me to explore the human experience through spontaneous and authentic imagery. I focus on documenting everyday life in urban environments, emphasizing mood, composition, and story. I believe in the power of visual design to shape perception and evoke emotion. Whether I’m behind the computer or the camera, I strive to create visuals that resonate, inspire, and deliver meaning with clarity.
